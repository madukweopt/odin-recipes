vegetable recipe

The vegetable soup is a popular delicacy consumed widely in Nigeria. It is more popular in the southern part of the country than the northern region.

INGREDIENTS REQUIRED TO PREPARE VEGETABLE SOUP

  • Meat or steak
  • vegetable leave
  • Stock fish
  • Palm oil
  • Maggi
  • Salt

METHOD OF PREPARATION

  1. Soak the dried fish in hot water for four hours.
  2. Wash and cut the meat into bite-size pieces.
  3. Transfer the meat into a pot and add the chopped onions, Maggi or Knorr cubes, salt and pepper to taste
