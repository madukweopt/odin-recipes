vegetable recipe

The vegetable soup is a popular delicacy consumed widely in Nigeria. It is more popular in the southern part of the country than the northern region.

INGREDIENTS REQUIRED TO PREPARE VEGETABLE SOUP

  • Meat or steak
  • Salt
  • Stock fish
  • Palm oil
  • Maggi
  • vegetable leaf

METHOD OF PREPARATION

  1. Soak dry fish in boiling hot water for 2 minutes, drain and rinse well with cold water. Add to the boiling meat and cook till meat is tender. The stock should be about 2 to 3 cups if it is more take out some and preserve.
  2. Clean snail, season with little salt and boil till a fork can barely pass through. Set aside (Snail should not be cooked till it is soft and floppy, it should still have a discernible crunch).
  3. To the cooked meat with 2 or 3 cups of stock, add palm oil, onions and pepper, cook for 5 minutes.
  4. Add, periwinkle, 1/2 cup of crayfish and seasoning cubes. Stir and taste, add salt.
  5. Cook for 10 minutes until palm oil loses its raw taste.
  6. Add waterleaf, ugu, and cooked snails, stir and cover, cook for 3 minutes.
  7. Add the remaining 2 tablespoons of crayfish and cook till vegetables have softened but is not mushy. Take off the heat and serve.
